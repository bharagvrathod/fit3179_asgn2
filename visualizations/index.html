Multiple Visualisations

This page demonstrates how you can use pure.css grid for your layout.

Category and Subscribers

Packed bubble chart depicting category-wise subscribers distribution. The Music category dominates with Entertainment being the second most subscribed category, just falling short of 1 billion subscribers. The two combined make more subscribers than all the other categories in the above 1 billion subscribers category.

Youtubers Geomap

A 3D map indicating country-wise YouTubers distribution depicting the number of YouTubers that originated from a specific country using the size of the channel over nodes. India, US, and Brazil make the top 3.

Layer Graph Subscribers and Earnings

It's a layered plot with dual axes, depicting interesting insights about YouTubers originating over different years, showing their max vs. min number of subscribers alongside the max of the highest yearly earnings vs. lowest yearly earnings among them. We can see that YouTubers who originated in 2016 have significantly more with quite higher max salary with lower max subscriber values compared to the rest of the years, while for YouTubers who originated in 2018 earn significantly less than the number of subscribers.

Bar Chart Top 50 YouTubers

It shows a nested bar chart showing the top 50 YouTubers divided based on their content category. We can see that the Music category dominates with the most number of subscribers compared to the rest. The Entertainment category, with fewer YouTubers, also dominates the rest except for Music, which indicates that entertainment content is the most growing content type on YouTube.
